Usage:
<-p or --port> <1-65536>                        listens on given port  
<-? or -h or --help>                            displays usage
<-! or --about>                                 displays about
<-v or --version>                               displays version
<-p or --port> <-e>                             listens on default port
<-p or --port> <-e> <PORT>                      listens on given alias
